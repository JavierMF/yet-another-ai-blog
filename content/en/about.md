The internet is saturated with AI content, and that's precisely what pushed me to create this blog. Too many hypesters, too many haters, too much FOMO. I can't believe everyone has such strong opinions on such slippery ground.

So, faced with that landscape, I'm going to try to build **an honest log of experiments, projects, tests, reflections and use cases** related to AI. Not just as a software engineer adapting to this tsunami in the industry, but also as someone genuinely interested in technology who tries to apply new developments in everyday life.

I'm [Javier Muñoz Ferrara](https://www.linkedin.com/in/javiermf).
